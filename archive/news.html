9/25/2014  Dodge Challenger SRT Hellcat specs

The Horsepower War has escalated! The Challenger fires a major salvo with new looks and more-powerful engines. The base 6.4-liter Hemi V-8 now makes 485 hp and 475 lb-ft of torque; choose either a six-speed manual or a new eight-speed automatic. The aptly named Hellcat features a supercharged 6.2-liter V-8 that makes 707 hp and 650 lb-ft of torque. In our testing, we hit 60 mph in 3.6 seconds and the quarter-mile in 11.7 seconds. Sadly, only the automatic transmission is available.
